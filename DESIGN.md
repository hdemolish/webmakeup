## Overview

High-end Editorial Makeup Artistry. The UI evokes a premium matte finish, resembling a contemporary physical magazine. It relies on sharp corners, expansive whitespace (negative space), and subtle micro-animations to convey luxury and sophistication.

## Colors

The palette avoids generic flat colors, opting for warm neutrals and deep ink to provide soft but strong contrast.

- **Primary (#1C1917):** Deep stone for main headings and strong borders. It feels softer and richer than pure #000000.
- **Secondary (#57534E):** Warm grey for descriptive body text, maintaining readability without the starkness of black.
- **Tertiary (#C5A880):** "Champagne" - used sparingly for buttons, active states, and decorative elements.
- **Neutral (#FAF9F6):** Alabaster background, providing a rich, paper-like feel.
- **Neutral Alt (#F2F0E9):** Used to distinguish alternating sections without using harsh borders.

## Typography

Combining a highly structured elegant serif for display with an airy sans-serif for body.
- **Cormorant Garamond:** High contrast serif for dramatic headings.
- **Montserrat:** Clean, airy, geometric sans-serif for modern readability.
- **Great Vibes:** Used exclusively for expressive, decorative script elements (like a signature).

## Layout & Animation

- Asymmetrical and overlapping layouts (Zig-zag) for services instead of standard grids.
- Masonry gallery to allow natural photo proportions.
- Smooth `fade-up` scroll animations (duration 0.8s, ease-out) to reveal content gracefully.
- Hover effects are subtle: images scale up by 3-5%, text might fade in, maintaining a relaxed, premium pace.
